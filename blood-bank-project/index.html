Core Features

Everything a blood bank needs, beautifully organized

Donor Management

Register, track, and manage donors with blood group filtering, availability status, and donation history.

Inventory Tracking

Real-time blood stock levels with expiry tracking, low-stock alerts, and batch management per blood group.

Hospital Requests

Hospitals submit blood requests with urgency levels. Track status from pending through approval to completion.

Donation Camps

Plan, organize, and track blood donation camps with registration counts and location management.

Urgent Alerts

Critical shortage warnings and urgent request notifications keep the team focused on what matters most.

Secure & Audited

Firebase Authentication with role-based access. Every action is logged for complete audit trails.

The Problem

Why traditional blood bank management fails

  • Paper-based records lead to lost donor information and delays in matching blood types during emergencies.
  • No expiry tracking means blood units often go to waste while shortages persist in other groups.
  • Manual hospital coordination via phone calls wastes critical time when patients need blood urgently.
  • Camp data is siloed — no central system to track registrations, expected turnout, or outcomes.
